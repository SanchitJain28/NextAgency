# How to Get Your Shopify Store Ready for the Holidays (Before It's Too Late)

Last year's Black Friday and Cyber Monday weekend: $11.5 billion in sales on Shopify alone.

24% increase from the previous year.

And most of that went to stores that prepared months in advance.

The stores that waited until November to "get ready"? They scrambled, missed opportunities, and watched competitors clean up.

Here's the truth: the holiday season isn't won in December. It's won in September and October when you're building gift bundles, setting up promo strategies, and optimizing your store for peak traffic.

I've helped dozens of Shopify stores prepare for holidays. The ones that follow this playbook? They do 40-60% of their annual revenue in Q4.

Here's exactly how to get your store ready.

## The Holiday Shopping Timeline (Start Earlier Than You Think)

Most store owners think holiday prep starts in November.

Wrong.

**Here's when shoppers actually start:**

- **September:** 21% of shoppers start browsing and planning
- **October:** Early bird deals, gift guide research
- **November:** Black Friday/Cyber Monday (peak buying)
- **December:** Last-minute shoppers, gift cards, express shipping

**Your prep timeline:**

**August-September: Foundation**
- Audit inventory
- Plan bundles and gift sets
- Design holiday landing pages
- Set up promo code strategy
- Update store theme with festive elements

**October: Launch**
- Launch early bird deals
- Publish gift guides
- Start email campaigns
- Test checkout flow
- Finalize shipping strategies

**November: Execute**
- Black Friday/Cyber Monday campaigns
- Daily email sequences
- Retargeting ads
- Live chat support ready
- Inventory management

**December: Close Strong**
- Last-minute gift messaging
- Gift card promotions
- Express shipping options
- Post-holiday sales planning

**January: Retain**
- Thank you emails
- Loyalty program invites
- Post-holiday sales
- Analyze what worked

If you're reading this and it's already November, don't panic. You can still execute parts of this. But next year? Start in August.

## Strategy #1: Create Irresistible Gift Bundles & Holiday Hampers

Gift bundles are the secret weapon of holiday sales.

**Why they work:**

1. **Higher AOV** - Bundle of 3 items at $75 vs single item at $30
2. **Simplify decisions** - "Perfect gift for Mom" beats "Figure it out yourself"
3. **Move inventory** - Bundle slow-moving items with bestsellers
4. **Stand out** - Everyone sells products. Not everyone sells curated gift experiences.

**Types of bundles to create:**

### 1. The "For [Person]" Bundle

Instead of making customers build their own gift, do it for them.

Examples:
- "For Him: The Complete Grooming Set" (beard oil + trimmer + balm)
- "For Her: Self-Care Essentials" (candle + bath bombs + face mask)
- "For Fitness Lovers" (resistance bands + water bottle + towel)
- "For the Home Chef" (spice set + apron + cookbook)

**How to price:**
- Calculate total individual price
- Offer 10-20% discount on bundle
- Example: Items total $85 individually ‚Üí Bundle price $70 (saves $15)

![Gift bundle product packaging](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1549465220-1a8b9238cd48?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

### 2. The "Complete the Look" Bundle

Perfect for fashion/apparel stores.

Example:
- "Winter Ready" (coat + scarf + gloves + beanie)
- "Date Night Outfit" (dress + shoes + clutch + jewelry)
- "Work From Home Comfort" (hoodie + joggers + slippers)

### 3. The "Bestseller Bundle"

Take your 3-4 top products and bundle them.

Example:
- "Customer Favorites Set - Our 3 Most-Loved Products"
- Social proof built-in (these are bestsellers for a reason)

### 4. The "Try Everything" Sample Bundle

Great for beauty, food, or beverage brands.

Example:
- "Coffee Lover's Sampler" (5 different coffee blends, 4oz each)
- "Skincare Discovery Set" (cleanser + toner + moisturizer in travel sizes)

### 5. The "Build Your Own" Bundle

Let customers choose 3, 4, or 5 items and get a discount.

Example:
- "Pick Any 3 for $99" (normally $40 each)
- Creates customization + discount motivation

![Holiday gift set arrangement](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1607344645866-009c7b3a1b57?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

**How to create bundles in Shopify:**

**Option 1: Shopify Bundles App (Free)**
- Install from Shopify App Store
- Create fixed bundles (you choose the products)
- Auto-syncs inventory
- Simple, clean, works out of box

**Option 2: Advanced Bundle Apps**
- Bold Bundles ($20-80/mo) - more customization
- Bundle Builder by Shophooks ($10-30/mo)
- Bundler ($7-20/mo)
- Better for complex bundles, volume discounts, tiered pricing

**Bundle presentation tips:**

**Product images:**
- Don't just show individual items
- Create a styled photo of the bundle together
- Show it wrapped or in a gift box
- Add "Everything included:" list with checkmarks

**Naming:**
- Be specific and gift-oriented
- ‚ùå "Bundle #1"
- ‚úì "The Cozy Night In Bundle - Perfect for Her"

**Descriptions:**
- Lead with who it's for
- List what's included (with individual prices)
- Show the savings
- Add gift messaging

Example:
"Treat Mom to a spa day at home. This curated set includes our bestselling lavender candle ($28), organic bath salts ($22), and silk sleep mask ($18). Normally $68 - now just $55. Arrives in a beautiful gift-ready box."

**Scarcity & urgency:**
- "Limited Holiday Edition - Only 100 Sets Available"
- "Order by Dec 20 for Christmas Delivery"
- "48 sets sold in last 24 hours"

## Strategy #2: Strategic Promo Code System

Random discounts don't work. Strategic ones do.

**The holiday promo code framework:**

### Early Bird Codes (October)

**Goal:** Capture early planners, reward loyal customers

**Examples:**
- EARLYBIRD20 - 20% off for early shoppers
- VIP25 - 25% off for email subscribers
- THANKYOU15 - 15% off for past customers

**Advantage:** Spreads out orders, reduces fulfillment crush in December

![Promo code discount strategy](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1607082348824-0a96f2a4b9da?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

### Black Friday/Cyber Monday Codes (November)

**Goal:** Maximum sales volume

**Examples:**
- BFCM30 - 30% off sitewide (biggest discount of year)
- FLASH40 - 40% off for 6 hours only (creates urgency)
- CYBER50 - 50% off select items (move specific inventory)

**Structure:**
- Friday: Best overall discount (30-40% off)
- Saturday: Category-specific (30% off outerwear)
- Sunday: Bundle deals (Buy 2 get 1 free)
- Monday: Final hours (35% off + free shipping)

**Stacking strategy:**
- Base discount + extra for bundles
- Example: "30% off everything + extra 10% off when you buy a bundle" = SAVE40BUNDLE

### Gift Giver Codes (December)

**Goal:** Make gifting easy and attractive

**Examples:**
- GIFTGIVER20 - 20% off orders $100+
- FREESHIP - Free shipping (no minimum)
- LASTMINUTE15 - 15% off + express shipping discount
- WRAPPING - Free gift wrapping (added value without cutting profit)

**Minimum thresholds:**
- HOLIDAY15 - 15% off orders $75+
- HOLIDAY20 - 20% off orders $125+
- HOLIDAY25 - 25% off orders $200+

Encourages higher cart values.

![Black Friday Cyber Monday deals](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1607082349566-187342175e2f?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

### Post-Holiday Codes (January)

**Goal:** Clear inventory, retain customers

**Examples:**
- THANKYOU30 - 30% off for holiday buyers
- NEWYEAR25 - 25% off sitewide (January sales)
- CLEARANCE50 - 50% off holiday items (clear seasonal stock)

**How to implement promo codes:**

**In Shopify Admin:**
1. Go to Discounts
2. Create discount code
3. Set type: Percentage, Fixed amount, or Free shipping
4. Set requirements: Minimum purchase, specific collections, customer groups
5. Set usage limits: One per customer, total usage limit
6. Set dates: Start and end times (auto-activate/deactivate)

**Pro tips:**

**Don't publicize all codes:**
- Some codes are for email subscribers only (creates value)
- Some codes are for cart abandoners (recovery)
- Some codes are for VIPs (loyalty reward)

**Stack strategically:**
- Bundle discount + promo code = powerful
- Free shipping + percentage off = high conversion

**Test discount levels:**
- Start conservatively (15-20%)
- Increase if not hitting targets (25-30%)
- BFCM can go higher (30-40%)

**Communicate clearly:**
- "Use code HOLIDAY20 for 20% off orders $75+"
- State minimums and exclusions upfront
- Show countdown timer for time-limited codes

## Strategy #5: Prepare for Peak Traffic & Orders

Nothing kills holiday sales faster than a crashed site or inventory running out.

### Site speed optimization

Holiday traffic = 3-5x normal. Your site needs to handle it.

**Audit now:**
- Run Google PageSpeed Insights
- Target: Under 3 seconds load time (mobile)
- If over 5 seconds, you're in trouble

**Quick fixes:**
- Compress images (use TinyIMG app)
- Remove unused apps (each app slows site)
- Upgrade hosting plan if needed
- Use Shopify's CDN (built-in)

![Website performance optimization](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1460925895917-afdab827c52f?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

### Inventory management

**Forecast demand:**
- Look at last year's holiday sales
- Add 20-30% buffer for growth
- Factor in new products

**Stock up early:**
- Order inventory by August/September
- Account for shipping delays
- Communicate with suppliers about lead times

**Low stock alerts:**
- Set up notifications when inventory drops below threshold
- Display "Only 3 left in stock" on product pages (creates urgency)
- Plan for restocks mid-season

**What to do if you run out:**

Don't just show "Out of Stock."

Options:
- "Back in stock by [date] - Pre-order now"
- "Join waitlist - we'll notify you"
- "Similar items you'll love" (recommend alternatives)

### Shipping strategy

**Shipping deadlines:**

Calculate and communicate clearly:
- Standard shipping: Order by Dec 18 for Christmas
- 2-Day shipping: Order by Dec 22 for Christmas
- Overnight: Order by Dec 23 for Christmas

**Display everywhere:**
- Homepage banner
- Product pages
- Cart page
- Checkout
- Email footer

![Shipping and delivery deadlines](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1566576721346-d4a3b4eaeb55?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

**Shipping options:**

Offer multiple:
- Standard (free over $X)
- Express (2-day)
- Overnight

Price express reasonably. Don't gouge last-minute shoppers‚Äîthey'll remember.

**Free shipping:**

Most powerful incentive during holidays.

Strategies:
- "Free shipping on orders $75+" (set threshold 20% above your AOV)
- "Free shipping on all gifts" (for gift bundles)
- "Free shipping Dec 1-24" (promotional period)

Show progress: "Add $12 more for free shipping!"

**International shipping:**

If you ship internationally:
- Add extra buffer for delivery estimates
- State customs delays possible
- Recommend ordering by early December

## Strategy #7: Payment Flexibility (Buy Now, Pay Later)

$18.2 billion in holiday sales used Buy Now, Pay Later last year.

If you don't offer it, you're losing sales.

**Why BNPL works during holidays:**

People want to give great gifts but have budgets. BNPL lets them:
- Buy $200 gift, pay $50/month for 4 months
- Split cost across paychecks
- Avoid credit card debt

![Buy now pay later payment options](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556742393-d75f468bfcb0?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

**BNPL options for Shopify:**

**Shop Pay Installments (built into Shopify):**
- Free for merchants
- Customers split into 4 payments
- Orders $50-3,000 eligible
- Most seamless option

**Afterpay:**
- 4 interest-free payments
- Popular with Gen Z and Millennials
- Integration easy

**Klarna:**
- 4 payments OR monthly financing
- Pink badge stands out
- Strong brand recognition

**Affirm:**
- Monthly payments for larger purchases
- Good for high-ticket items ($500+)
- Clear terms

**How to implement:**

1. Enable in Shopify Payments settings
2. Add BNPL badges to product pages
   - "Or 4 payments of $25 with Afterpay"
   - Show calculation automatically
3. Mention in emails
   - "Give the gift they'll love. Pay over time with Afterpay."
4. Highlight on bundles
   - "$150 bundle = 4 payments of $37.50"

**Results:**

Stores that add BNPL see:
- 20-30% increase in AOV
- 10-15% increase in conversion rate
- Lower cart abandonment

It's literally free money (for you). Just enable it.

## Strategy #8: Post-Holiday Strategy (Retain Those Customers)

The holiday season doesn't end December 25. Smart stores plan for January too.

**Post-holiday tactics:**

### Thank you emails

Send within 24 hours of delivery:
- "Thank you for your order!"
- Ask for review (incentivize with 10% off next order)
- Include care instructions or tips for product

### Feedback requests

2 weeks after purchase:
- "How's your [product]?"
- Link to review form
- Offer to resolve issues

![Customer retention strategy](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556761175-4b46a572b786?q=80&w=2070&auto=format&fit=crop)

### Post-holiday sale

December 26 - January:
- "After Christmas Sale - 30% Off"
- Position as clearing inventory
- Make room for new products

### Returns & exchanges

Extend holiday return window:
- "All holiday purchases can be returned through January 31"
- Builds trust
- Encourages buying (less risk)

Handle returns gracefully:
- Easy return process (pre-paid label)
- Fast refunds
- Offer exchanges or store credit first

### Loyalty program invites

Convert one-time holiday buyers into repeats:
- "Join our VIP program - get 15% off your next order"
- "Earn points on every purchase"
- "Early access to sales"

You can also consider implementing [subscription options](/blog/best-shopify-subscription-apps-2026-comparison) for products that work well as recurring purchases.

### Re-engagement campaigns

February-March:
- "We miss you! Here's 20% off"
- "New arrivals just for you"
- Segment: holiday buyers who haven't returned

## Common Holiday Mistakes to Avoid

### Mistake #1: Waiting until November to prep

By then, it's too late for inventory, bundles, and proper planning. Start in August.

### Mistake #2: Not having shipping deadlines

Customers need to know when to order. If you don't tell them, they'll order late and blame you.

### Mistake #3: Running out of bestsellers

Nothing worse than "Sorry, sold out" during peak season. Stock deep on winners.

### Mistake #4: Terrible mobile experience

70% of traffic is mobile. If your site sucks on phones, you're losing 70% of potential sales.

### Mistake #5: No gift options

No gift wrapping, no gift message option, no bundles = missed opportunity. Make gifting EASY.

### Mistake #6: Generic messaging

"Holiday sale" is boring. "Stress-free gifts for everyone on your list" is specific and helpful.

### Mistake #7: Ignoring email

Email drives 20-30% of holiday revenue. If you're not sending daily emails during BFCM, you're leaving money on the table.

### Mistake #8: Forgetting post-holiday

The sale doesn't end December 25. Plan for returns, January sales, and retaining those new customers.

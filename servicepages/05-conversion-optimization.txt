## Real Results: Home Goods Brand CRO

A home decor brand had good traffic (25K monthly) but low conversion (1.3%). They were spending $15K/month on ads.

### Key Issues Found
- Product pages missing key information
- 82% cart abandonment
- Mobile experience confusing
- No trust signals
- Slow page load (5.8s)

### Improvements Made
- Added detailed product descriptions and dimensions
- Simplified checkout process
- Redesigned mobile product pages
- Added customer reviews prominently
- Optimized images (5.8s → 2.1s load)

### Results After 3 Months
- Conversion: 1.3% → 2.6% (+100%)
- Cart abandonment: 82% → 68%
- Mobile conversion: +142%
- AOV: +14%
- Revenue: $32K → $58K/month (+81%)

**ROI:** $35K CRO investment. Additional $26K monthly revenue = $312K annually

**How long before we see results?**
Quick wins in 2-4 weeks. Full program shows significant results in 3-6 months. CRO is iterative and continuous.

**What conversion rate should we aim for?**
Industry average is 1-3%. Good stores achieve 3-5%. Exceptional stores 5%+. Depends on traffic quality, AOV, and product type.

**Will CRO work for our niche?**
Yes. CRO principles apply across industries, though specific tactics vary by product type and audience.

**Do you guarantee results?**
No guarantees, but we only recommend changes with proven track records. Average clients see 30-60% conversion improvement.

**How does A/B testing work?**
We show different versions to different visitors, measure which performs better, and implement the winner.

**Can we implement recommendations ourselves?**
Yes. Audit includes implementation guidance. Most clients prefer us to implement for speed and technical expertise.
